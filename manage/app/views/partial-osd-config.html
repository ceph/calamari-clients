CLUSTER SETTINGS

Cluster Wide OSD Settings

Please be aware, settings on this screen affect the entire cluster. This may cause unexpected behavior, including data-loss, and prevent normal operation of service.

Pause

Disable IO requests to all OSDs in cluster.

No Backfill

Disable backfill operations on cluster.

No Out

Prevent Down OSDs from being marked as out.

No Deep Scrub

Disables automatic periodic deep scrub operations on OSDs. May still be initiated on demand.

No Scrub

Disables automatic periodic scrub operations on OSDs. May still be initiated on demand.

No In

Prevent OSDs from booting OSDs from being marked as IN. Will cause cluster health to be set to WARNING.

Be advised the No in flag prevents OSDs from being marked as in on boot and any OSDs that started with the flag set will remain that way until restarted.

No Up

Prevent OSDs from automatically getting marked as Up by the monitors. This setting is useful for troubleshooting flapping OSDs.

No Recover

Disable replication of Placement Groups.

No Down

Prevent OSDs from automatically getting marked as Down by the monitors. This setting is useful for troubleshooting flapping OSDs.
